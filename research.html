I am primarly interested in analytically approaching partial differential equations through the tools of functional analysis and variational methods including Quasi-variational inequalites traditional variational inequalities. My current research concerns reconciling a system of interdependent quasi-variational inequalites through a connection to the N-membrane problem. This system is further complicated through coupling to a system of elliptic partial differential equations connected over a contact region via a characteristic function. The formulation additionally incorporates uncertainty which requires analysis over Bochner spaces in order to minimize expected loss in application.

Additionally, I am interested in semigroup structures of solutions and well as operator algebras.
